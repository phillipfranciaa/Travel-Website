Paris, France Travel Package (3 Days / 2 Nights)

 

Inclusions

  • Accommodation:
    • 2 nights stay in a 4-star hotel in central Paris (double occupancy).
    • Daily buffet breakfast.
  • Transfers:
    • Roundtrip airport transfers (airport-hotel-airport).
  • Tours & Activities:
    • Half-day guided Paris city tour (Day 1).
    • Skip-the-line tickets to the Eiffel Tower (Day 1).
    • Seine River cruise tickets (Day 2).
    • Guided day tour to Versailles (Day 3).
  • Meals:
    • Lunch during the Versailles tour (Day 3).
  • Transportation:
    • Metro tickets for easy transportation within Paris.
    • Roundtrip transportation to Versailles.
  • Entrance Fees:
    • Entrance to the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Palace of Versailles.
  • Tour Guide:
    • English-speaking licensed guide for the city tour and Versailles tour.
  • Travel Insurance:
    • Basic travel insurance covering the duration of the trip.
  • Souvenir: Complimentary Paris-themed souvenir.

Exclusions

  • Airfare: International flights to and from Paris.
  • Personal Expenses:
    • Meals and drinks not specified in the itinerary.
    • Personal shopping, souvenirs, and gratuities.
  • Optional Activities:
    • Additional tours or excursions not mentioned in the itinerary.
  • Travel Documentation:
    • Visa fees (if required) and passport costs.
  • Upgraded Travel Insurance:
    • Comprehensive insurance for extra coverage beyond the basic package.
  • Other Expenses:
    • Additional hotel charges such as room service, minibar, and laundry.

Itinerary

Day 1: Discover Paris

  • Morning:
    • Arrival at Charles de Gaulle Airport.
    • Private transfer to your 4-star hotel in the city center.
    • Check-in and relax.
  • Lunch: On your own, at a recommended café near the hotel.
  • Afternoon:
    • Paris City Tour: Explore the Champs-Élysées, Arc de Triomphe, and the Latin Quarter with your guide.
    • Visit Eiffel Tower with skip-the-line access to the second floor for stunning views of the city.
  • Evening:
    • Free time to explore Montmartre and visit the Sacré-Cœur Basilica.
    • Dinner (on your own) at a local bistro in Montmartre or the Latin Quarter.

Day 2: Museums & Seine River Cruise

  • Morning:
    • Hotel breakfast.
    • Visit the Louvre Museum and explore its world-famous art collections, including the Mona Lisa and Venus de Milo.
    • Free time for shopping or strolling through Jardin des Tuileries.
  • Lunch: On your own, near the Louvre or by the Seine River.
  • Afternoon: Free time to explore Le Marais district or visit the Musée d'Orsay.
  • Evening:
    • Enjoy a scenic Seine River Cruise, where you’ll see landmarks like Notre-Dame Cathedral, the Eiffel Tower, and the Musée d'Orsay from the water.
    • Dinner (on your own) at a riverside restaurant or a charming brasserie.

Day 3: Versailles Palace Day Trip

  • Morning:
    • Hotel breakfast.
    • Depart for a guided day tour to the Palace of Versailles.
  • Activities:
    • Explore the grandeur of the Palace, including the Hall of Mirrors, the King’s Apartments, and the exquisite gardens.
  • Lunch: Included at a restaurant near the Palace.
  • Afternoon:
    • Free time to walk around the Versailles Gardens or rent a bike/boat to explore the Grand Canal area.
  • Evening:
    • Return to Paris and transfer to the airport for your departure.
